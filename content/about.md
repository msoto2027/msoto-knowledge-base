## About Me

Hello! My name is Marisol, and I am a senior at Texas Tech University with a passion for fitness, music, reading, and discovering new experiences. I enjoy staying active, learning about health and wellness, attending concerts, and spending time with family and friends. I am always looking for opportunities to grow, challenge myself, and develop healthy habits that contribute to a balanced lifestyle.

My interest in fitness inspired me to create **FitIQ Knowledge Base**, a website designed to provide reliable information and resources for individuals at all stages of their fitness journey. Recently, my brother and I decided to restart our fitness journey together before he heads off to Texas Tech this fall. This experience motivated me to learn more about exercise, nutrition, and overall wellness, while creating a resource that could help others pursue their own health and fitness goals.
