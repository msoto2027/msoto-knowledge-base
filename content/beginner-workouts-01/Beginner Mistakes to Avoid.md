# Beginner Mistakes to Avoid

These are mistakes I’ve personally made every time I’ve tried getting back into working out.

## The big ones

- Doing way too much on day one
- Skipping rest days because I “feel fine”
- Thinking soreness = progress
- Quitting after missing one workout
- Overcomplicating everything

## What I’m learning

Consistency is way more important than intensity. I’d rather do 30 minutes three times a week than burn out after a “perfect” week.

> “Missing one workout doesn’t break progress—quitting does.”
